What is Intelligent Learning?
Intelligent Learning (TM) is the world's first professional online platform connecting ambitious students with like-minded tutors in an effort to achieve a mutually-beneficial teacher-student relationship. Whether you're a university/high school student seeking academic support for a particular course, or a tutor seeking to further your career in teaching and earn some money, we've got you covered! Intelligent Learning is a free platform with the goal of helping the community excel academically! Sign up today!

What is the monthly membership cost?
We do not currently charge a fee to our users! All features listed are currently free of cost. However, if you wish to support us, you may make donations by transferring money directly to Abhi Kapoor's bank account with RBC.

What benefits do you offer for Tutors?
At Intelligent Learning, we believe that Tutors are at the core of our business. If you are a tutor looking to gain valuable experience and create a meaningful positive impact on a student, whilst earning some money, please sign up! For further information regarding mentorship opportunities for tutors, please send us an email at tutorrelations@intelligentlearning.com

What career options are currently available with Intelligent Learning?
At Intelligent Learning, we have a strong commitment to diversity and inclusion. We proud ourselves on being a leader by hiring individuals from all backgrounds, and we do not discriminate on the basis of color, religion, ethnicity, nationality, or sexual orientation. If you think you'd be a good fit for one of our job openings, please email us your CV and Resume at careers@intelligentlearning.com
